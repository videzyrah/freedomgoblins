FREEDOM GOBLINS

Bailing random people out of jail

"Freedom Goblins" was the fake non-profit organization that youtube content creator Goblin used when attempting to bail random people out of jail in Los Angeles and Las Vegas.

His attempts seemed to be somewhat in vain, however @cduerkseems to have had some alternatively inspirational results applying the same concept

Bailing random people out of jail may appear to be simply a social media attention getter that is dangerous

However googling 'corruption of the cash bail system' returns an AI overview that helps put things into perspective:

Cash bail has corrupted the justice system in several ways, including:

  • Economic injustice

    • >Cash bail creates a two-tiered system of justice where wealth determines who is released from jail. People who can't afford bail remain incarcerated, while those who can pay are released.

  • Racial disparities

    • Black and Latino defendants are often assigned higher bail amounts than white defendants with similar charges. This leads to higher rates of pretrial detention for people of color.

  • Profit Driven Industry

    • Bail bond companies charge a nonrefundable fee to cover the bail amount for a defendant. This creates a profit-driven industry that extracts money from low-income Americans.
